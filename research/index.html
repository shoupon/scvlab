Research projects

Multi-stack Architecture

Intelligent vehicle system is complicated and a good architecture to start with will help us manage the complexity. We propose a multiple stack layered architecture that divides the functions according to different ways of interaction with the world and also has top-down feature with well-defined interfaces in between. This architecture enables us using model checking and conformance testing technique on intelligent vehicles.

Model checking

It is not sufficient to demonstrate that collaborative driving systems work. Before we allow these systems on public highways we must prove that these systems will do no harm even when rare, multiple failures occur. The failures will include loss of communications, failures or inaccuracies of sensors, mechanical failures of the automobile, aggressive drivers who are not participating in the protocol, and unusual obstacles or events on the roadways. To verify that such systems are safe, we apply the techniques developed for communications protocols.
